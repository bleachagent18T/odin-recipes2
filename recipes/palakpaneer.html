About:

Palak Paneer is a popular North Indian vegetarian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a thick, spiced puree of spinach (palak). The dish typically includes ingredients like garlic, ginger, green chilies, and garam masala, and is known for its vibrant green color and creamy texture. It’s commonly served with roti, naan, or rice. Palak paneer is rich in iron, protein, and calcium, making it both nutritious and flavorful. It's a staple in Punjabi cuisine and widely enjoyed across India and in Indian restaurants worldwide.

Ingredients:

    Spinach (palak)
  • Paneer (cubed)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chilies
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Water
  • Fresh cream (optional, for richness)
  • Kasuri methi (optional, for aroma)
  • Lemon juice or amchur (optional, for tang)

Steps to prepare Palak Paneer:

  1. Wash spinach thoroughly and blanch it in hot water for 2–3 minutes
  2. Immediately transfer spinach to ice-cold water to retain green color.
  3. Drain and blend spinach into a smooth puree (with green chilies if
    4. desired).
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  5. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  6. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and oil separates.
  8. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt; cook for 1–2 minutes.
  9. Add the spinach puree and cook for 4–5 minutes on medium heat.
  10. Add garam masala and mix well.
  11. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  12. Optionally, add a spoon of fresh cream and crushed kasuri methi.
  13. Adjust salt and tang with lemon juice or amchur if needed.
  14. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
